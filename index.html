Traffic Light Instructions

Click the STOP button to turn on the red light.This signals that all movement should come to a complete stop.

Click the READY... button to activate the yellow light.This warns that the light is about to change — slow down and prepare.

Click the GO! button to switch on the green light.This means it is safe to move forward.
